Geek Out
The event is mostly based on your enthusiasm and will to speak your mind. Be creative, be vocal and you are sure to bag the prize! You could form a group with 2-3 members or could attend the event like a lone wolf. You need not be Shashi Tharoor or Shakespeare to have fun at this event!- great news, huh?!
Rules
The event has 3 rounds and you are required to go through the first 2 rounds to have your own ted talk(yeah, for real)
   Round 1: Turn coat
   Round 2: Lexical hunt
   Round 3: Ted talk
Turn coat rules:
  •     You are required to speak for and against the motion for 1 minute(every member of the group will be given a minute)
  •    The topic will be given on the spot and feel free to express your opinions(General topics will be given)

    • Lexical hunt:
     Bring in your dictionaries and explore its pages with this unique attraction event.  A difficult word will be given on the spot and you are required to search for its meaning and say an appropriate synonym.

    Ted talk:
      With a general topic given on spot, the stage is yours, the focus is on your words and as always it's your turn to shine!- basically give your own ted talk
